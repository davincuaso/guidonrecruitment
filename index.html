Writing Cluster
About the staff
At the forefront of The GUIDON, News is committed to report stories that are transparent, thorough, and relevant to every Atenean. Writing for News entails documenting the Ateneo’s most pressing issues on paper and online to consistently keep the community up to date.

Tasked with unmasking the truth behind campus events, News staffers are expected to think critically and creatively to produce significant and accurate stories.

Inquiry
Writing Cluster
About the staff
Inquiry is the analytical and investigative arm of The GUIDON, tasked with looking into the heart of current events and unearthing Ateneo’s hidden truths. Our staff tackles the more controversial issues that afflict the Atenean community while deepening campus discourse through investigative, data-driven journalism.

Through staffers’ critical reportage, Inquiry provides a platform for Ateneo’s unheard voices. As the staff produces various analyses on campus issues, Inquiry staffers must be analytical, innovative, and most importantly, insightful.

Vantage Magazine
Writing Cluster
About the staff
We are The GUIDON’s online magazine: A publication dedicated to campus culture and the people behind it. Predicated on the endless potential of digital media, we take journalism into the 21st century—away from pretense and towards accessibility. Vantage Magazine highlights the latest trends in food, film, music, and theater and the arts through relevant and engaging content.

Our staff aims to promote a two-way conversation between the publication and our readers through our stories of campus culture and other student interests. With a firm grasp of multimedia storytelling and all things Ateneo, Vantage Magazine celebrates creative expression and responsible storytelling.

Research
Organizational Cluster
About the staff
As the analytical arm of The GUIDON, Research staffers conduct data-driven studies to craft strategies that address the publication’s challenges during press and non-press cycles.

There are two divisions under the Research Staff: Analysts and writers. Analysts develop strategies for the publication, examine readership behavior, and collaborate with other staffs to improve their respective internal processes. On the other hand, writers compose research-heavy reports that add depth and credibility to stories.

Researchers must not only be well-versed in quantitative and qualitative analyses, but also in telling stories through numbers through the strategic usage of data to produce plausible solutions and content that will boost the publication’s efforts to delve Deeper into the Truth.

Design Cluster
About the staff
The GUIDON’s Video Production staff spearheads the publication’s shift to multimedia journalism. Through dynamic visual storytelling methods, the staff explores a variety of interests that range from hard news to soft news and everything in between. Composed of Content Strategists and Technical Producers, teams within the staff work hand-in-hand to produce documentaries, video articles, and event highlights to pave the way for engaging with audiences within and beyond the Ateneo.

The staff prides itself as storytellers that champion the distinct harmony between journalistic competence, technical artistry, and most importantly, a heart for stories.

Digital Development
Design Cluster
About the staff
The Digital Development staff innovates Philippine campus journalism to produce engaging interactive articles that are unique to The GUIDON. In collaboration with the Writing Cluster and the Design Cluster, the staff aims to provide readers with an immersive experience that creatively combines text and multimedia. Aside from creating immersive content, one of the staff’s primary responsibilities is to maintain The GUIDON’s website and to make it a more usable online platform. The staff is also expected to improve The GUIDON’s efficiency by automating tasks that are time-consuming for human resources to handle.

Despite being relatively new, the staff paves the way for an efficient and cutting-edge modernization of The GUIDON as the publication swiftly moves towards the technological front of online storytelling.
